About BrailleSync

BrailleSync is a minimalist tool designed to accurately translate text into Braille patterns instantly. Inspired by the clean aesthetics of modern utility apps, it focuses strictly on functionality without the clutter.

Supported Languages:

  • English: Standard uncontracted (Grade 1) Braille.
  • Hindi: Utilizing Devanagari Bharati Braille standards.
  • Telugu: Utilizing Telugu script Bharati Braille standards.

Everything you translate is saved locally on your device ensuring absolute privacy. No data is sent to external servers.
